Cool Stuff on the Asus

Here are some things I tinkered with.

Using AirDroid

I installed AirDroid, an amazing free app that allows you to connect to and control your Android device remotely using a web browser. With AirDroid, you can:

  • Wirelessly transfer stuff to and from your Android.
  • Enjoy using your computer's monitor and keyboard to do SMS messaging.
  • Locate and lock your Android remotely when it's lost, and even snap a picture of the thief!

For more information, including how to install and use AirDroid, go to airdroid.com

Accessing My Mongo DB Project

It was a thrill to run my Mongo DB project in Cloud9 from my laptop and then use a browser on the Asus tablet to access the website. I was able to see how it displays on a 7-inch mobile device and I could make changes to the database almost as easily as from a laptop or PC.
