About Me

I grew up in Manila, in a family of well-respected physicians who set the bar pretty darn high for me.

On the road to a career in the Biological sciences, I moved to San Francisco, California

to complete my undergraduate studies, and then worked for many years as a Microbiologist at

Guittard Chocolate Company in Burlingame.

Discovering a knack and a love for programming led me to reinvent myself as a Computer Scientist.

What excites me most is the ability to develop sophisticated, elegant, and novel solutions to complex

common software application problems.
